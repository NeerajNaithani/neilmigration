Canada PR from Australia includes the application of PR from Australia for Canada. This process requires the calculation of points for Canada PR which are based on the Express Entry system. This system calculates the comprehensive ranking score of a visa aspirant which include his points as per his age, experience, and education. English proficiency is also important to get a good CRS score. The main program for immigration of professionals is the Federal skilled worker program. This program requires someone to have experience of 1 year to be deemed acceptable as per it. That's why the Federal skilled worker program selects professionals for immigration to Canada. The experience of a visa aspirant is also ranked as per the importance of his occupational profile. For example, visa aspirants in certain profiles such as legislators are given more importance(points) than those who have experience in executive profiles.
